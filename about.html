About Me

Hello! I'm faun5.

I'm a 21-year-old Cuban-American digital artist with a deep love for character creation and vibrant illustration. I've been drawing for well over a decade, starting with doodles in notebooks that grew into a passion for digital painting and storytelling through art.

My work often centers around animals, expressive characters, and saturated colors. Growing up online, I was immersed in a world of creativity and community. From niche forums to art-sharing sites, the internet shaped my imagination. I strive to reflect that playful, collaborative spirit in everything I make.

Thank you for stopping by my portfolio! I hope my work brings a little joy your way. Feel free to explore my gallery or reach out if you're interested in a commission.
